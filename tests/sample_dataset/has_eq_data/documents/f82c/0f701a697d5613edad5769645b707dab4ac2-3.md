we make the substitution  s = (1 - r^n)/(1 + r^n), and of the resulting integral

\[
4 \int_0^1 (e^{-as} - e^{-a/s})(1 - s)^{-1 + 1/n}(1 + s)^{-1 - 1/n} ds
\]

we discard everything except the portion over (0, a^{1/2}). We may then replace the algebraic factors by a constant, and the quantity to be determined is greater than

\[
K_3 \int_0^{a^{1/2}} (e^{-a} - e^{-a/s}) ds.
\]

Consider separately each of the intervals [(j-1)a, ja] (j = 1, 2, \cdots, [a^{-1/2}]). Since the minimum of the integrand in the jth interval is

\[
e^{-a} - e^{-a/ j} > -a + j^{-1} - j^{-2},
\]

the value of the integral is greater than

\[
a \sum_{j=1}^{[a^{-1/2}]} [-a + j^{-1} - j^{-2}] > K_4 a |\log a|.
\]

Now, for k = 2, 3, \cdots, let a_k = k^{-1}(\log k)^{-3/2}, and let

\[
f(z) = \exp \left( - \sum a_k \frac{1 + z^n k}{1 - z^n k} \right).
\]

If n_k \to \infty fast enough, then f again has the desired properties.
