### Purpose of ACWDL 오류 수정:
The purpose of the ACWDL error correction is to adjust the Family Medical Benefit Amount (FMBA) based on the revised amount.

### Method of Recording ACWDL 18-13:
- Deleted terms are indicated with strikethrough.
- New terms are underlined and bolded.

### Purpose of the Army Welfare and Discharge Office (ACWDL) Order:
This document aims to provide updated FMBA amounts to the Army in accordance with Section 1924(d) of the Social Security Act. The FMBA amount is used to determine the portion that the family member can receive from the LTC (Long Term Care) benefits.

### FMBA Amount for LTC Beneficiaries:
For the period from July 1, 2018, to June 30, 2019, the FMBA amount for LTC beneficiaries (Community Caregiver and Family Member of LTC beneficiary, referring to Work Sheet B, Medical Condition MC 176W (05/08), IX, A.2, and B.2) is 2,057.50. The FMBA amount for 2017 - 2018 was 2,030.

### Implementation Plan:
The National Welfare System (SAWS) will implement programming changes for the 2018 FMBA amount during the next SAWS release.
