# National Safety Council

## First Aid Training Process
(1 session - 4-hour course)
- **Validity Period:** 3 years
  - Emergency situation handling
  - First aid principles
  - Emergency call and road closure
  - Practice test

## Basic Life Support Training Process
(1 session - 3-hour course)
- **Validity Period:** 2 years
  - Emergency response principles
  - Child injury prevention
  - Emergency call and road closure
  - Adult/child/infant
  - Practice test

## Recertification Modules

### Bus Driver
- Example: If the driver completed their training in April 2010, they must complete the recertification modules starting from April 2014 and finishing by April 2015. If the completion date is not confirmed, contact the training office at 571-252-1720. Module 2 "Basic Driving" will be assigned after completing module 1.
- Example: If the driver completed their training in April 2010, they must complete recertification module 4 starting from April 2014 and finishing by April 2015. If the completion date is not confirmed, contact the training office at 571-252-1720.
