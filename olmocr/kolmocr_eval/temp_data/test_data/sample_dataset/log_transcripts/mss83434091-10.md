Head Qurs U.S. Forces.  
Brownsville Texas July 6th 1865  

Special Orders  
No. 18  

Par III Surgeon C. G. G. Merrell 22nd U.S.C. Troops  
is hereby detached from his Regiment and  
assigned temporarily to duty in charge Post  
Hospital Brownsville Texas.  

By Order Of  
Brig Maj Genl. Giles A Smith  
A. D. Wain Jr.  
Asst. Adjt. Genl.  

Surgeon C. G. G. Merrell  
22nd U.S.C.T.
